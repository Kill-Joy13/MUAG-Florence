A Study of Florence by Mountains: The Impact Of Sound And Visuals In Immersive Storytelling.


I will explore the emotional themes and visual motifs of the game Florence by Mountains.

The game Florence uses many design techniques to help envelope the player into the game. Moreover, with these techniques, the game becomes an experience rather than an activity.

The first of which I will be discussing is the use of color. In the game, Florence's color is the most significant indicator of mood and tone.
The game makers used a variety of palettes from start to finish that helped the viewer explore and understand the storyline
and the protagonist's' inner feelings. The protagonist, Florence, says mental health is directly reflected within the color scheme of the world around her.
This, I believe, is an excellent example of Masumi’s effect. As players and humans, color directly affects our psychology.
With these sudden and dramatic color shifts, we are instantly reacting, though we do not quite know what until after the chapter has played out.

The second I will discuss is sound and, in this instance, music. In the game, Florence's music is a huge aspect of creating an environment and the plot.
As one of the main characters is a musician, we can follow not only the protagonist but also Krish through life and through their innermost feelings
that are expressed through the color palette and the music choice.

The music in this game was created chapter by chapter like a soundtrack for each leg of the story; the similar yet different tones and rhythms allow for a
soundscape that truly engulfs you in the story. Moreover, It helps create more authentic and gradual changes in affect and then emotion, much like in horror movies.
When you hear those sinister tonal changes start to play, your body instantly knows something is to come far before your mind knows what exactly to be afraid of.
Moreover, that is precisely what is happening here but on a subtler scale.

These narrative tools along with others allow for quite a simple puzzle game to turn into an emotional and almost therapeutic experience.
The storyline and theme of this game is of one many of us can relate to being stuck in a job and in a place in life feeling very uninspired
with the enormous of life really weighing us down to meeting people and having them in our lives for a short time but those experiences negative
and positive help move us forward if we allow them to. In this way and with this somewhat repetitive Game that mirrors life in a way of waking up brushing
your teeth working waking up brushing your teeth working and so on we are able to live out our choices in life in a short amount of time.
